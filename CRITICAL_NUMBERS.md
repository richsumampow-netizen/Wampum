# Critical Numbers & Timeframes Quick Reference

**Use this document to memorize all the important numbers for the Michigan Credit Insurance Producer Exam.**

---

## GRACE PERIODS (When you can be late paying premium)

### Life Insurance
- **Standard Grace Period: 31 days**

### Health Insurance (Varies by premium mode)
- **Weekly premiums: 7 days**
- **Monthly premiums: 10 days**
- **Quarterly: 31 days**
- **Semi-Annual: 31 days**
- **Annual: 31 days**

**Memory Trick:** More frequent payments = shorter grace period (Weekly 7 → Monthly 10 → All others 31)

---

## CLAIM PROCESSING TIMEFRAMES (THE 20-15-90-60 RULE) ⭐⭐⭐

**THIS IS THE MOST IMPORTANT SEQUENCE ON THE EXAM**

| Step | What Happens | Timeline |
|------|--------------|----------|
| 1 | Customer gives Notice of Claim | **20 days** |
| 2 | Company provides claim forms | **15 days** after notice |
| 3 | Customer submits Proof of Loss | **90 days** after loss |
| 4 | Company pays claim | **60 days** after proof (or "promptly") |

**Memory Device:** **20-15-90-60**
- 20 = Notice
- 15 = Forms
- 90 = Proof
- 60 = Payment

**MEMORIZE THIS ABOVE ALL ELSE!**

---

## REINSTATEMENT WINDOWS

### Health Insurance Reinstatement
- Must apply within: **45 days** after lapse
- After 45 days: Reinstatement right is lost

### Legal Actions (Health Insurance)
- Cannot sue until: **90 days** after submitting proof of loss

---

## LIFE INSURANCE SPECIFIC

### Free Look Period
- **10 days** to cancel and get full refund

### Suicide Exclusion
- **2 years** = NO coverage for suicide within 2 years
- After 2 years = Suicide IS covered

### Grace Period (Life)
- **31 days**

---

## CREDIT INSURANCE SPECIFIC

### Certificate of Insurance Delivery
- **Within 30 days** of incurring the debt (closed-end loans)

### Credit Life Suicide Exclusion
- **2 years** = NO coverage within 2 years
- After 2 years = Covered

### Loan Coverage Extension (Michigan Rule)
- Can extend **15 days** beyond maturity date maximum
- Once loan paid off: Insurance ends (max 15 days later)

### Aggregate Provisions
- **90-day maximum limit** on total benefits in any 3-month period

---

## MICHIGAN LICENSING

### Minimum Age
- **18 years old**

### Pre-Licensing Education

**Full Producer License:**
- **40 hours total**
  - 34 hours insurance content
  - 6 hours Ethics/Regulatory

**Life or Health Insurance Only:**
- **20 hours total**
  - 14 hours insurance content
  - 6 hours Ethics/Regulatory

**Ethics/Regulatory:**
- **6 hours** (required for ANY license type, both pre-licensing and CE)

### Continuing Education (CE)

**Renewal Cycle:** Every 2 years

**Required Hours:** **24 hours every 2 years**
- Must include **3 hours of Ethics/Regulatory**
- Remaining 21 hours can be any approved topics

**Memory Trick:** **40 hours = Producer** | **20 hours = Life/Health Only** | **6 hours = Ethics (always)** | **24 hours CE every 2 years (3 must be Ethics)**

---

## APPOINTMENTS AND TERMINATION

### New Appointment Filing
- Company must file with DIFS within: **15 days** of agency agreement

### Insurer Termination Notice
- Company must notify DIFS within: **30 days** of terminating you

### Agent Termination Notice
- Agent must notify DIFS within: **15 days** of self-terminating

### Address Change Notification
- Must notify DIFS within: **30 days** of address change

**Memory Trick:** **Appointment 15** | **Insurer terminates 30** | **Agent terminates 15** | **Address change 30**

---

## CONTROLLED BUSINESS LIMIT

### The 15% Rule ⭐
- Cannot exceed **15% of total premiums** in a **12-month period**

**Calculation:**
- Total premiums written: $100,000
- 15% of that: $15,000
- Maximum controlled business: $15,000

---

## GUARANTY ASSOCIATION LIMITS

### Michigan Life and Health Guaranty Association

**Total Benefits Limit per Policy:**
- **$300,000 maximum** for all policy benefits

**Cash Value Limit per Policy:**
- **$100,000 maximum** in cash value

**Important:** These are SEPARATE limits (not combined)

---

## PENALTIES AND FINES

### Per Violation Fines

| Circumstance | Fine |
|-------------|------|
| **Unknown/Not Discovered** | **$1,000** |
| **Known/Discovered** | **$5,000** |

### Cease and Desist Violation
- **$20,000** (much higher because you ignored official order)

### Insurance Fraud (Felony)
- Prison: Up to **4 years**
- Fine: Up to **$50,000**

---

## MICHIGAN-SPECIFIC RULES

### Age 70 Protection ⭐
- Cannot refuse to insure **SOLELY** because person is age 70
- CAN charge more for age
- CAN use age in rate calculations
- But CANNOT deny based solely on age

### Commissioner Term
- **4 years** (appointed by Governor)

### Credit Insurance Coverage Limits
- Cannot exceed: **Total indebtedness**
- Extends beyond maturity: **15 days maximum**
