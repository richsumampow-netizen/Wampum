# Michigan Credit Insurance Producer Exam Study Guide

A comprehensive, step-by-step study guide for the Michigan DIFS Credit Insurance Producer License Exam.

## Quick Facts

- **Total Questions:** 90
- **Time Limit:** 3 hours
- **Passing Score:** ~70% (63+ questions)
- **Study Time:** 12-13 hours

## Exam Breakdown

- Phase 1: General Insurance Fundamentals (35 questions)
- Phase 2: Credit Insurance Specifics (30 questions)
- Phase 3: Michigan Insurance Regulations (25 questions)
